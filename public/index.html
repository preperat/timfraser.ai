About Tim Fraser

Tim is a senior technology leader with over 20 years of experience across cloud architecture, DevOps, cyber security, data platforms, and software delivery.

He has led major transformations across industries including engineering, maritime, AI/ML, enterprise SaaS, and automation.

More recently, Tim served as Cloud Lead at OMC International, a Melbourne-based marine engineering company renowned for its DUKC® (Dynamic Under Keel Clearance) decision-support system — often described as “air traffic control for ships”. He modernised and operated the cloud platforms underpinning real‑time depth, tide, and vessel data to enable safer, deeper-draught transits and higher port throughput.

Tim helps organisations transition from reactive IT to proactive, data-driven decision-making — with a calm, practical approach that removes noise and delivers clarity.

Earlier in his career, Tim founded and operated Relax Internet, an independent ISP in Melbourne in the early 2000s. Running dial‑up and early broadband infrastructure end‑to‑end — routing, BGP, mail, DNS, and billing — gave him a deep, hands‑on grounding in Linux and open‑source systems that continues to inform his engineering approach today.
